Who we are

Built for data explorers

Livekolab is a web-based data visualization platform designed to make analytical insights accessible to everyone. Upload any CSV file, select your columns, apply filters, and watch your data come to life as an interactive chart — all in seconds.

Our mission

Insights without barriers

Our mission is to empower individuals and teams to uncover patterns in their data without needing specialized tools or technical skills. By offering intuitive controls, smart column-type detection, and instant visual feedback, we make data analysis feel effortless.

Features

Everything you need

Bar Charts

Compare values across categories with adaptive, dynamically colored bars and sort controls.

Pie Charts

Visualize proportional distributions with auto-generated color schemes and interactive tooltips.

Filtering

Drill into specific subsets of your data using column-level filters without reloading the file.

Smart Type Detection

Automatically detects numeric vs categorical columns to apply the right aggregation logic.

Secure Auth

Firebase-powered authentication keeps your account safe with email and password login.

Responsive Design
